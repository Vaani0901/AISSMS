Seat of Green

Crafting Sustainable Outdoor Living!

Welcome to Seat of Green, where we are committed to transforming metal waste into stylish and durable outdoor furniture. Based in Pune, our company specializes in collecting metal waste and upcycling it into high-quality chairs, benches, and other seating options, offering eco-friendly alternatives for outdoor spaces.

At Seat of Green, we believe in the power of upcycling to reduce waste and protect the environment. By collecting metal waste and turning it into functional and attractive outdoor furniture, we not only minimize the impact on landfills but also provide a sustainable option for outdoor seating. Our furniture is designed to be both stylish and environmentally friendly, making it ideal for eco-conscious consumers.

Our process begins with the careful collection and sorting of metal waste, which is then cleaned and transformed into chairs, benches, and other seating options. Each piece of furniture is crafted with precision and care, ensuring that it meets our high standards of quality and sustainability. Our furniture is designed to withstand the elements, providing long-lasting and eco-friendly seating solutions for outdoor spaces.

Join us in our mission to create a more sustainable outdoor living environment. Choose Seat of Green for outdoor furniture that not only enhances your outdoor space but also helps protect the planet. Together, we can create a greener future, one seat at a time.
