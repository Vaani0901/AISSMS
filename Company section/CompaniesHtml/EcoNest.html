EcoNest

Creating comfort from waste!

At EcoNest, we're on a mission to revolutionize pet care by transforming discarded paper into cozy and sustainable bedding for small animals. Based in the vibrant city of Pune, our environmentally conscious company is dedicated to reducing waste while providing comfort to our furry friends.

We believe in the power of upcycling, which is why we collect paper waste from various sources, including offices, schools, and households, to give it a new purpose. By upcycling paper into pet bedding, we not only reduce the amount of waste sent to landfills but also contribute to a cleaner, greener environment for everyone.

Each bedding product at EcoNest is carefully crafted with love and dedication. Our team ensures that every piece of paper is cleaned, sanitized, and transformed into soft, fluffy bedding that small animals like hamsters, guinea pigs, and rabbits will adore. Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond our products. We prioritize ethical practices in our business operations, ensuring fair wages and working conditions for our employees. Additionally, all our packaging is made from recycled materials, further reducing our carbon footprint.

Join us in creating a world where waste is repurposed into something beautiful and beneficial. Choose EcoNest for eco-friendly, pet-safe bedding that's as comfortable as it is conscientious.
